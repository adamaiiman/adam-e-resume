Weekly Training

My friends and I usually playing badminton at the UiTM Kedah Sports Unit every Monday and Tuesday. These sessions help improve our skills, keep our bodies healthy, and serve as a great way to relieve daily stress while training together with friends.

Tournament

"Sukan Kolej (SUKOL)"

During my first semester, I represented Kolej Malinja B in the college-level badminton tournament. Although I broke my ankle during the competition, I still managed to secure a bronze medal in the men's doubles category.

"Sukan Antara Skuad (SUKAS)"

The "SUKAS" competition was held twice during my time at UiTM Kedah. In my third semester, I achieved first place after defeating an opponent from the senior squad. In the following semester, I managed to secure second place.

"Sukan SUKSIS Utara (SUTRA)"

The "SUTRA" brought together five universities—UUM, UniMAP, UPSI, USM, and UiTM—in a single multi-sport event. I represented UiTM Kedah in the men’s doubles badminton category during my second semester and successfully earned a silver medal in the tournament, which was hosted at UUM.
