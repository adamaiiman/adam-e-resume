SUKSIS Activities

Weekly Activities

Morning Roll Call

The morning roll call requires all members to assemble at the mini stadium as early as 6:45 AM, fully dressed in complete uniform. This routine instills discipline, punctuality, and readiness before beginning the day’s training activities.

Physical Training

Every Thursday, we gather at the HEP building parking area at 4:45 PM for physical training. Our routines include a 2.4 km jog followed by various sports activities designed to improve stamina, teamwork, and overall fitness.

Foot-Drill Training

We assemble at the UiTM Kedah parade square (in front of Dewan Perdana) at 6:45 AM for foot-drill training, which continues until 12:00 PM. The session resumes at 2:30 PM and concludes at 5:00 PM every Friday.

SUKSIS Program

"Kursus Etika dan Pengurusan Integriti"

The course was conducted at the Police Basic Training School in Sungai Petani for five days, from 25th to 29th August 2024. This course strengthened the bond among squad members and enhanced personal ethical values and integrity.

"Kursus Seni Mempertahankan Diri & T-Baton"

The course was held from 26th April to 1st May 2024 at the HEP Square and the UiTM Kedah Sports Unit. This training enhanced students’ self-defense skills, improved physical readiness, and strengthened discipline through hands-on T-baton handling sessions

"Kursus Menembak & Pengurusan Senjata Api"

The course was conducted on 25th August at the IPD Pendang shooting range. This training provided exposure to firearm safety management, proper weapon handling procedures, and practical shooting experience, helping to build confidence and responsibility when operating firearms.

"Kursus Kawad Senjata"

The course was held from 28 September to 2 October at the Kedah Police Headquarters in Alor Setar. This training strengthened our discipline and coordination, enhanced weapon-handling skills, and improved our precision and synchronization during armed marching drills.

"Kursus Asas Pasukan Gerakan Am"

The course was conducted from 18 to 22 August at Battalion 3 Camp in Bidor, Perak. This training provided exposure to basic General Operations Force skills, strengthened teamwork and endurance, and helped improve field readiness through hands-on tactical exercises.
